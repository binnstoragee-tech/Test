Privacy Policy

01 — SCOPE

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, information gathered automatically as you use the site, and information related to the orders and inquiries you place with Outright Trading.

  • Account details — your name, mobile number, email address, and password when you register for an account.
  • Business details — company name and business type, for customers ordering on a wholesale or business account.
  • Order & inquiry information — products viewed, cart contents, quotation requests, and order history.
  • Communication data — messages sent through our chat, contact forms, or customer support channels.
  • Technical data — IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited, collected automatically through your browser.
02 — PURPOSE

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate the platform and serve your orders, specifically to:

  • Create and manage your account, and verify your identity when you log in.
  • Process inquiries, quotations, and orders, and communicate with you about their status.
  • Respond to questions sent through live chat or our contact channels.
  • Improve site performance, product listings, and the overall shopping experience.
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, or security issues.
03 — YOUR ACCOUNT

Accounts & Login

Logging in with your mobile number or email and a password lets us keep your order history, saved quotations, and business details tied to one place. Your password is stored in encrypted form — we cannot see or recover it, so if you forget it, you'll need to reset it through the Forgot password link on the login screen.

Keep your login details private. Contact us immediately if you believe your account has been accessed without your permission.

04 — COOKIES

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember items in your cart, and understand how the site is used so we can improve it. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though some parts of the site — such as staying logged in or checking out — may not work properly without them.

05 — THIRD PARTIES

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share it in the following situations:

  • Service providers — trusted vendors who help us run the platform, such as hosting, payment, and delivery partners, bound to use your data only to provide that service.
  • Legal requirements — where disclosure is required to comply with the law, respond to a valid legal request, or protect the rights and safety of Outright Trading and our customers.
  • Business transfers — in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, customer information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
06 — SAFEGUARDS

Data Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure, including encrypted password storage and access controls on our internal systems. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, so while we work to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

07 — YOUR CHOICES

Your Rights & Choices

You can review and update your account details at any time from your profile page. You may also:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request that your account and associated data be deleted, subject to any records we're required to keep for legal or accounting purposes.

To make any of these requests, reach out through the contact details below.

08 — RETENTION

Data Retention

We retain your account and order information for as long as your account remains active, and for a reasonable period afterward to meet our legal, accounting, and business record-keeping requirements. When information is no longer needed for these purposes, we take steps to securely delete or anonymize it.

09 — AGE LIMIT

Children's Privacy

Outright Trading is a wholesale platform intended for business and retail customers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children, and our services are not directed at individuals under the age of 18.

10 — UPDATES

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal and regulatory reasons. When we make material changes, we'll update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
